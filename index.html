About Us

-- Who are we --

Fresh-Gro   is India's biggest grocery store online sustenance. With more than 1500 items and over 75 brands on our list, you will discover all that you are searching for.

From new Fruits and Vegetables, Rice and Lentils, Spices and Seasonings to Packaged items, Beverages, Personal consideration items - we have everything.

We are committed to providing wholesome products that fit your lifestyle.

Since, we are the best, we provide the best

-- What we do --


We offer door to door delivery directly from farm to your home.

Connecting farmers and consumers so that farm fresh produce directly reaches your home without any adulteration and the involvement of any middlemen.

We Inspire People to Make Everyday Eating Extraordinary > For over 35 years we've made it our objective to separate ourselves from the rest.
